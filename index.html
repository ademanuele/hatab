About Me

Hello. My name is Arthur and I am a self-taught woodworker, native to Malta. I have been pursuing this craft properly for the past 3 years but have been interested in it from a very young age.

The best reward the craft offers is in the joy that is brought to anyone receiving one of my pieces. I would be happy to hear from you if you have an idea that you would like to bring to life.
